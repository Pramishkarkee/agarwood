COMPANY GOALS AND OBJECTIVES

Birta Agro Concerns’ primary goal is to promote agarwood and sandalwood plantations in Nepal due to their high economic value in the international market, to support the agricultural community, and to create more employment opportunities for youth in order to reduce migration and support the national economy. Some specific objectives of the company are as follows:
Plantation: Birta Agro Concerns will be supporting the interested farmers and agricultural communities in Agarwood and Sandalwood plantation. The support will be provided in the form of technical guidance by agriculture experts, allocating essential resources including seedlings, irrigation facilities, agarwood inoculation kits, etc. as well as investment support.
Nursery: For small farms, Birta Agro Concerns will be offering seedling to the farmers. On the other hand, for large scale farms, we can assist communities and farmers to develop their own Agarwood and Sandalwood nurseries. For the same, we will be providing the seeds, fertilizers, manures, other resources as well as technical guidance by our team of experts.
Harvesting: Along with plantation and other processes, Birta Agro Concerns will be helping the farm owners and communities with successful harvesting of the plantations. Be it training labor for harvesting, selection of working techniques and methods, minimization of post-harvesting losses, Birta will be assisting the associated stakeholders for all technical concerns on ground.
Processing: It is the step preceding harvesting of plantations. Birta Argo Concerns will be assisting the farmers and communities with the processing of the woods and related plant products to turn those into high demand commercial products for international markets.
Quality Control: Birta Agro Concerns will also support the farmers and agricultural communities with quality control of the plantations. Our agriculture expert’s team will help the farmers and communities to produce higher quantity and superior quality agarwood and sandalwood via means of inoculation and optimal utilization of manures, fertilizers, pesticides and farm management techniques.
Research and Innovation: Our Research & Development team is dedicated to the enhancement of agarwood and sandalwood plantation yield, concentrating on features such as volume, stem straightness, branching, wind stiffness, and wood quality, as well as choosing plants that can respond to rising environmental variability such as frosts and drought. We do and fund research in a range of stewardship areas, including as endangered species population surveys and the restoration of damaged sites and vital ecosystems.
