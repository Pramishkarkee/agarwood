Birta Agro Concerns is offering a wide range of investment and collaboration opportunities to the public, organizations and sales agents.

Investment opportunities for individuals and organizations:

  • Investment in cooperative plantation group:
    Birta Agro Concerns invites individuals and organizations to invest in our cooperative group plantation plans and reap up to 21- 30% yearly profit, which keeps increasing with every passing year till the plantation is harvested.
  • Investment in Agarwood inoculation:
    Birta Agro Concerns invites individuals and organizations to invest in our agarwood inoculation plans and earn yearly profits up to 21%.
  • Investment in 5 years old sandalwood plantation:
    Birta Agro Concerns invites individuals and organizations to invest in our existing 5 year old sandalwood plantation and start earning a 19% yearly profit after the plantation completes 15 years.

Collaboration opportunities for Agents:

Birta Agro Concerns invites sales agents to collaborate with us on profit percentage basis as per the sales achieved. We have two collaboration opportunities for sales agents as follows:

  • Sales agent for Agarwood products
  • Sales agents for tree sales to individuals and companies
