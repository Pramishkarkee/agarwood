Inoculation of Agar Wood:

Natural process of agarwood production i.e. natural fungal infection in Aquiralia tree doesn’t ensure high quality and quantity of agarwood. Thus, inoculation approach has been developed by researchers and widely employed by farm owners to serve the purpose since it is most successful and dependable way for increasing agar output. Commercialization efforts are underway, with the goal of making the technology available for widespread usage in the field. Birta Agro Concerns will be providing agarwood inoculation kits, training farmers using the same and on-site

agarwood inoculation service (in case farm owners or small farmers chose not to buy the kits due to financial restraint).

Inoculant production and sales:

Birta Agro Concerns has a tie-up with Z Black Diamond Agarwood LLP, Assam, India. Thus, Birta is an authorized distributor of agarwood inoculant kits in Nepal. Birta Agro Concerns will be supplying inoculation kits, inoculation chemical and other associated accessories to farms and agricultural communities.

Soil Test for Agarwood:

Birta Agro Concerns will be providing soil tests for agar wood plantation. Our expert(s) will conduct a site visit which will result in development of a comprehensive soil profile to understand the suitability of a piece of land for agarwood plantation. The profile will include an in-depth investigated soil conditions of a piece of land, including attributes physical qualities, chemical properties, and nutrient content. Based on expert’s opinion and own willingness, farm owners can take an informed decision regarding initiation of the plantation.

Agarwood quality test:

Agarwood comes in a variety of grades, as does its essential oil. They are exchanged in international market at various prices based on their quality. The chemical compositions of agarwood vary from one another, affecting their quality. Birta Agro Concerns will be assisting farm owners and agricultural communities to test the agarwood quality produced in their farms. This will help the duo, the farm owner(s) and agriculture expert(s) to develop a reasonable price structure to sell the products in international market.
